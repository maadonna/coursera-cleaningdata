## Codebook for Getting and Cleaning Data assignment

There was not a single codebook for the original data. It is a combination of the following files, all available from the source data:
https://d396qusza40orc.cloudfront.net/getdata%2Fprojectfiles%2FUCI%20HAR%20Dataset.zip
* activity_labels.txt
* features_info.txt
* features.txt

The assignment here contains the following data:

### Who
Numbered list of 30 subjects

### What
Activities:
* WALKING
* WALKING_UPSTAIRS
* WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
* SITTING
* STANDING
* LAYING
